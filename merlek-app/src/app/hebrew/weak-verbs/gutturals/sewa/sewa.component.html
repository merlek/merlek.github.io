Shewa

1 Vocal Shewa always becomes composite Shewa

שָׁאּ ֲ לוּ

Weak

קָטּ ְ לוּ

Strong

2 Silent Shewa in accented syllable Not composite

שָׁ לַ֫ חְתִי

Weak

קָ טַ֫ לְתִי

Strong

  • 3 Pe-Gutturals that are syllable-final (Hif, Hof, Qal Impf, Nif Perf, Nif Ptcp)
    Remember Theme Vowels

    Nif Perf 3ms
    • composite instead of silent sewa
    • performative takes corresponding vowel
    • a-class theme vowel

    נ ֶ א ֱ מ ַ ן

    Weak

    נ ִ ק ְ ט ַ ל

    Strong

    Qal Impf 3ms
    • composite instead of silent sewa
    • performative takes corresponding vowel
    • o-class theme vowel

    י ַ ע ֲ מ ֹ ד

    Weak

    י ִ ק ְ ט ֹ ל

    Strong

    BUT...
    ה or ח may or may not become composite!

    יַ חְ מֹךְ

    Weak

    יִקְטֹל

    Strong

  • 4 Special Case: Pe-Guttural that closes a syllable (w/ silent sewa) and would be followed by a vocal sewa)
    Composite sewa cannot precede vocal sewa

    Nif Perf 3fs
    • silent sewa becomes short vowel
    • vocal sewa becomes silent
    נִקְטְלָה
    נֶ עֱמְ דָה
    נֶ עֶמְ דָה
